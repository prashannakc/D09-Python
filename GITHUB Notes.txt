Terminologies:
1. local: Laptop
2. Remote: Server(GitLab/GitHub)
3. Remote url: where saved(url)
4. ssh-keygen: generate public/private key(connect local and remote)only your laptop can access the git
5. repo-repository
6. 

(Cryptography): (private/public key)
public key: encrypt
private key: decrypt
